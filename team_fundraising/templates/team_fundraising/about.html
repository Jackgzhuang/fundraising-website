Welcome to the Triple Crown for Heart donation site!


The Triple Crown for Heart is an annual group ride up the three North Shore mountains in Vancouver, British Columbia.

We are also a fundraising ride for two worthwhile charities, the BC Children's Hospital Foundation and the Children's Heart Network. As such, we are hoping our riders take the time to raise some money for these worthwhile causes.

If you are a rider, please click "Join" to set up an account and begin fundraising.

If you are someone supporting a rider, please find them in the Home screen, click on their page and then click "Donate."
