Triple Crown For Heart is a fun road riding event that raises funds in support of the BC Children's Hospital Foundation and the Children's Heart Network!

This year marks our 10th anniversary ride, we are raising $10,000 to send families living with congenital or acquired heart disease to Camp Zajac, as well as providing the remaining funds to the BC Children's Heart Foundation programs. We are hoping to raise $30,000 this year. Read more in our announcement.
